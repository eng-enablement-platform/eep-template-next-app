Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to explore the Engineering Enablement Platform. This is the EEP full-stack Next.js starter template and its documentation. Whether you are here to use the template, understand the thinking behind it, or contribute to it, you are in the right place.

As agentic development becomes more prevalent our abstractions become higher. Rather than holding every feature implementation in our heads, we operate more like architects. But that only works when the foundation is solid. You should always be able to reason about the shape of the system, even when you are not reading every line. That is what this template is built to give you.

## What you will find here

- **[The Stack](/docs/the-stack)** covers every technology in the template and why it is here
- **[Architecture](/docs/architecture/overview)** explains the three-layer system and how a request moves through it
- **[Conventions](/docs/conventions/code-style)** covers coding standards, naming rules, and testing approach
- **[Dev Environment](/docs/dev-environment/local-setup)** covers local setup, the dev container, auth, and the database
