# 👋 Hey there!

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to explore the Engineering Enablement Platform (EEP), a suite of
tools designed to help people learn and build projects in a repeatable, standardised, and structured way!

As [agentic engineering](https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/agentic-engineering) becomes more prevalent our abstractions become higher.
When building projects rather than holding every feature implementation in our heads, we operate more
like architects. But that only works when the foundations are solid. The goal should be a
codebase you can always navigate by its shape, not by having to read everything.
This template and accompanying documentation is built to give you exactly that!

<Callout type='idea'>
  There are many different ways to approach software projects and nothing here
  represents a hard rule or the only "right" way to do something. Feel free to
  pick and chose from the ideas and conventions listed as you see fit. I'd also
  highly encourage everyone go through the scaffolding exercise themselves and
  find out exactly what does and doesn't work for you. Have fun coding!
</Callout>
