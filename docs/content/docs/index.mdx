# 👋 Hey there!

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to explore the Engineering Enablement Platform (EEP), a suite of
tools designed to help engineers build projects in a repeatable, standardised, and structured way.

As agentic development becomes more prevalent our abstractions become higher.
Rather than holding every feature implementation in our heads, we operate more
like architects. But that only works when the foundation is solid. The goal is a
codebase you can always navigate by its shape, not by having to read everything.
This template is built to give you exactly that.

<Callout>
  The patterns here are tried and tested and for the vast majority of use cases
  will have you covered. Where there are limitations, or different architectures
  would suit you better, those are called out too.
</Callout>
